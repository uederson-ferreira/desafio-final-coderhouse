EPISODE I:
THE PHANTOM MENACE

Turmoil has engulfed the Galactic Republic. The taxation of
trade routes to outlying star systems is in dispute. Hoping
to resolve the matter with a blockade of deadly
battleships, the greedy Trade Federation has stopped all
shipping to the small planet of Naboo. While the Congress
of the Republic endlessly debates this alarming chain of
events, the Supreme Chancellor has secretly dispatched
two Jedi Knights, the guardians of peace and justice in the
galaxy, to settle the conflict....
